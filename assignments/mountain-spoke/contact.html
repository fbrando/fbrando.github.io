Bangtail Divide

Length: 12 miles
Duration: 3 to 5 hours (one-way)
Difficulty: Easy - Moderate

Built in 2003, the Bangtail Divide Trail (BDT) quickly became the local go-to trail for those looking for a long ride without a long drive. The BDT offers easy access to a ridgeline traverse with stellar views of multiple mountain ranges, fields of wildflowers and well-crafted singletrack sections guaranteed to satisfy.

Shafthouse

Length: 8.88 miles
Duration: 2 hours (one-way)
Difficulty: Advanced

Featuring incredible vistas of the Northern Bridger Range, Shafthouse is a must for those seeking an exhilarating ride with amazing views. The initial steep ascent is a small price to pay for the following 2,000-foot descent on this Bozeman classic.
